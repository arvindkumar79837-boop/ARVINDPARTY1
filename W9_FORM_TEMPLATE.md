## Additional Information for Secretary of State Office

**Documents typically required:**
- Completed W-9 form
- Government-issued photo ID
- Business registration documents (if applicable)
- Proof of address

**Filing Instructions:**
1. Complete all sections of the W-9 form.
2. Sign and date the form.
3. Submit to your state's Secretary of State office along with any required supporting documents.
4. Keep a copy for your records.
